Elegant Crystal Candle Warmer Lamp with Timer and Dimmer

Enhance your living space with this beautiful and safe crystal candle warmer lamp. Perfect for creating a warm, inviting ambiance without the risks of open flames, this modern design blends style with practicality for everyday use or special occasions.

This exquisite candle warmer features 120 K9 crystals and beads, arranged in a flowing tassel-like design that catches and reflects light beautifully. With 3 halogen bulbs, it provides a flame-free, clean, and safe alternative to traditional candles, allowing your favorite scents to shine without smoke or soot.

The adjustable height ensures that your jar candles sit securely and melt evenly, while the dimmer control lets you customize the lighting and scent dispersion to suit your mood. A built-in timer with 2/4/8-hour auto shut-off offers added convenience and safety, especially when you're away or planning for nighttime use.

Its mirror-polished electroplated top and chic marble base add a touch of luxury and sophistication to any decor. Durable and scratch-resistant, this candle warmer is built to last, maintaining its elegant appearance over time.

Whether you're looking to create a cozy atmosphere in your home or office, or searching for a meaningful gift for birthdays, Christmas, or other special events, this crystal candle lamp is the perfect choice.
