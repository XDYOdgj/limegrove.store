Rite Lite Plush Chanukah Menorah Toy

Celebrate the spirit of Chanukah with this adorable plush menorah designed specifically for young children. It combines fun and education to help teach the traditions of the holiday in a safe, engaging way. The soft and durable plush construction ensures that kids can play with it without any risk of injury, making it a perfect addition to any festive season.

Each night, children can add a candle to the menorah, learning about the eight nights of Chanukah in an interactive and meaningful manner. The set includes 9 bright, colorful candles that are labeled with numbers for each night, simplifying the celebration process. A convenient drawstring pouch is also included to keep the candles organized and stored safely.

Whether you're looking for a gift, a classroom teaching aid, or a way to add holiday cheer to your home, this plush menorah is an ideal choice. Its charming design brings a festive touch to any room while encouraging cultural awareness and creativity in young learners.
