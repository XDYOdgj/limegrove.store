Seraphic Glass Hurricane Candle Holders

Elevate your interior style with these modern, elegant, and functional candle holders that blend beauty with practicality.

Crafted from high-quality, durable glass, these hurricane candle holders bring a touch of sophistication to your living space. Standing at 9.75 inches tall, they're designed to make a striking visual impact, whether placed on a mantel, side table, or as a centerpiece in your dining room.

The unique design of the Seraphic holders features a 4.5-inch opening at the top that narrows to 3.5 inches at the base, making them adaptable for different candle sizes. While we recommend using 2-inch diameter candles, these holders are versatile enough to fit your preferred style and size.

Additionally, the holders are ideal for personalizing your decor with decorative rocks. We suggest using white polished 3/8-inch rocks for a sleek and clean look, though they are not included. This allows you to add a warm, inviting ambiance and create a unique, customizable decoration experience.

Whether you're aiming to enhance your home's aesthetic, setting the mood for a cozy evening, or adding an elegant touch to your table, these Seraphic candle holders are the perfect solution. A must-have for anyone who values both style and functionality in their home decor.
